This Tiny Assistant demo is based on Esri's ArcGIS Maps SDK for JavaScript AI Assistant component sample , using the Change in wheat production 2017-2022 web map.

ArcGIS AI Assistant can navigate the map, answer questions about the wheat production layers, and help inspect the data through chat.

The chat opens after signing in with an eligible ArcGIS Online organization account where AI assistants are enabled and your role can use them.

Weekend AI experiment: can a Codex/ChatGPT-style "pet" become a tiny assistant that replaces the standard ArcGIS AI Assistant panel?

Tip: right-click the assistant character to open a small menu, including an option to follow your cursor.
